Stations Growth


  • Pattern: Citi Bike is expanding steadily every year, especially during summer.
  • There is always a significant jump in the number of stations from June to September (summer months).
  • During other seasons, the number of stations seems to be steady.
  • This Growth is captured nicely by the animation above: in 2014 there was no station above central park area, but by the end of 2016 they are already reached the north part of Central Park.
  • Citibike is planning to expand more in the coming years.
